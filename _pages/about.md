I am Linlong Xu, a master's student in Molecular Engineering at the University of Chicago. Previously, I earned my bachelor's degree in Materials Science and Engineering from the University of Wisconsin–Eau Claire.

My academic interests lie in computational materials science, electrochemistry, and battery-related research. I am particularly interested in using computational methods to study functional materials and to understand their behavior in energy storage applications.
